# 🪶 Aesopus — Learn Through Story
Humans don't learn language effectively by memorizing sterile flashcards. We learn best through context, emotion, and narrative. Aesopus is an interactive, AI-powered web application designed to weave your target vocabulary into dynamic, living stories, allowing you to acquire new words naturally.

By placing vocabulary into branching, interactive narratives, Aesopus bridges the gap between rote memorization and true reading comprehension.

## ✨ Key Features
#### Context-Driven Acquisition
Input the words you want to learn, and the AI engine drafts a unique, multi-scene story in your target language that natively incorporates your vocabulary. As you read, the words are highlighted, allowing you to deduce their meaning from the surrounding text.

#### Personal Lexicon & Focused Generation
Build a long-term library of knowledge. Aesopus stores your words, tracking them as you interact with them and moving them from your "Personal Queue" to "Mastered." You can upload your own .txt files or prompt the built-in AI to instantly generate highly focused wordlists tailored to your specific interests (e.g., "kitchen vocabulary in German").

#### Public Archives
Not sure where to start? Browse the Public Archives to discover curated, community wordlists. Filter by language and CEFR proficiency level (A1–C2), and instantly import them into your personal learning queue.
